OLX Indonesia is an online classifieds platform (OLX.co.id) covering a wide range of categories including vehicles, property, electronics, home goods, jobs, and hobbies. It facilitates buying and selling, particularly of used cars, often with financing options. A specific search on OLX.co.id revealed "Supra" vehicles for sale in Jakarta Selatan, predominantly newer Toyota Supras with a few exceptions, priced between Rp 985,000,000 and Rp 1,799,000,000.  OLX News Indonesia (olx.co.id/news) provides news and articles related to automotive, technology, lifestyle, business, and events, including product reviews, buying guides, and promotional content. Recent articles include topics such as EV adoption, new car and gadget releases, fashion trends, and financial advice.
